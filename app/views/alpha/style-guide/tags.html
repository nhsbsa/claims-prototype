Claim

Not started

Complete

Treatment

In progress
The treatment could not be auto validated by the system and a Claims Officer is looking into it
Accepted
The treatment was either auto accepted or manually accepted by a Claims Officer
Contested
The treatment has been contested by a Claims Officer
Withdrawn
The treatment has been contested by a Claims Officer and the MS has since withdrawn the treatment from the claim
